Meet Sharon

You deserve to feel heard

I know how much courage it takes simply to land on this page. When you're struggling, reaching out can feel like the hardest part — so the first thing I want you to know is that here, there's nothing you have to fix, perform, or get right.

I'm Sharon, a BACP-accredited counsellor and psychotherapist in Brockham, Surrey, working with adults and young people aged 14+. Having experienced first-hand how much the right support can change things, I'll offer you a warm, non-judgemental space to be truly heard — and we'll go at a pace that feels right for you.

Your path forward

Starting is simpler than you think

Reaching out can feel like a big step, so here's exactly what happens — no pressure, and no surprises.

  1. 1

    Reach out

    Send a message or give me a call. It's completely confidential, and you're under no obligation to book anything.

  2. 2

    Have a free chat

    We'll talk for 30 minutes, free of charge, so you can share what's going on and see whether we feel like the right fit.

  3. 3

    Start feeling supported

    When you're ready, we'll agree a regular time that suits you and begin — usually weekly — at a pace that feels right.

What's possible

Imagine feeling like yourself again

Change rarely happens overnight — but with the right support, things really can shift. The people I work with often find their way back to:

  • Sleeping more easily, with a mind that finally quietens down.
  • Pausing and choosing how you respond, instead of spiralling.
  • Feeling steadier on hard days, knowing they won't swallow you whole.
  • Speaking to yourself more kindly, and setting boundaries without guilt.
  • Feeling connected again — to the people you love, and to yourself.
